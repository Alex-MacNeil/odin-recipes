Lasagna

This is the best lasagna. Please take my word for it.

Ingredients

  • Beans.
  • More Beans!
  • The beans of your enemies.
  • Beans from outer space.

Steps

  1. Don't show fear, the beans can smell it.
  2. Coddle your beans closely and whisper to them how much you love them. You do not have to really love the beans, but this may help.
  3. Boil on low for many months until the lasagna sprouts from the bean eggs.
  4. Consume.
